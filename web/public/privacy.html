Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Paternoster Media Agency ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the InstaEdit application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your social media accounts through InstaEdit, we collect:

  • Account information: your name, username, profile picture, and platform user ID from the connected platform (e.g., Facebook/Instagram).
  • Authentication tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to publish content on your behalf.
  • Content metadata: information about posts you create and publish through our platform (captions, media URLs, publishing timestamps).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your identity and link your social media accounts.
  • Publish content (videos, images, text) to your connected social media platforms as instructed by you.
  • Maintain and improve the App's functionality.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-256-GCM). We store your data on secure cloud infrastructure with access limited to essential operations personnel.

4. Third-Party Services

InstaEdit integrates with third-party platforms (Meta/Facebook/Instagram, TikTok, X/Twitter, YouTube). When you use these integrations, the respective platform's privacy policy also applies. We encourage you to review their policies:

  • Meta Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • X (Twitter) Privacy Policy
  • Google/YouTube Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information and OAuth tokens for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data — see our Data Deletion page for instructions.
  • Revoke OAuth permissions at any time through the respective platform's settings.
